Find & Snatch Unicorns

Mobile GPS

Use your mobile device GPS to locate, snatch, and train virtual Unicorns! You'll find Unicorns practically anywhere; whether it's in your hometown, or away to a far off destination on holiday.

Gain XP

Navigate safely around your city with fellow Bronies to grow your herd. The larger your team's Unicorn herd grows, the more XP you earn!

Outdoor Fun

Playing Unicorn Attractor helps to promote outdoor physical activity. Skip the gym today to go explore your hometown while snatching Unicorns!

Magic Mysteries

Once you've snatched enough Unicorns to build a herd, you'll be able to train the herd to perform powerful magic! Share magical secrets with members of your team to gain more XP.

Unicorn Secrets

Learn unique secrets in the app in order to snatch Unicorns before they 'wink out' or teleport back to Equestria.

Rare Alicorns

Do you have what it takes to find the elusive Alicorn? Alicorns are winged Unicorns; they have wings like a Pegasus, and a horn like a Unicorn. There aren't many Alicorns, so to find one will gain you significant XP!

Did You Know?

In Celtic mythology the unicorn was a symbol of purity and innocence, as well as masculinity and power. Tales of dominance and chivalry associated with the unicorn may be why it was chosen as Scotland’s national animal.
